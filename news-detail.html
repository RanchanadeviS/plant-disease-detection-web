What happened while using Field flyer?

Field Flyer is a special drone that helps farmers find and fix problems with their crops. It uses thermal sensors to take pictures of the fields. These pictures show temperature changes in the plants, which can be signs of disease. .

The drone flies over large areas of farmland on its own, following a set path. Farmers can control it with a simple app on their phones. They get instant updates and detailed reports about their crops' health. .

The drone is lightweight and strong, so it can be used in different weather conditions. It has GPS to navigate accurately and map out problem areas. This information can be combined with other farm tools to give a complete picture of crop health. .

In short, Field Flyer is a smart tool that uses AI and drone technology to help farmers keep their crops healthy and improve their harvests.
